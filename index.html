  •                                             Easy Peasy 10 Minute Rice                                        

    1 part rice (White or Basmati) to 2 parts cold water in a medium to large pan.
                            Bring to the boil uncovered (electric or gas, not induction hob).
                            Once boiling turn heat down to the lowest temperature and quickly place lid on.
                            Perfect rice in 10 minutes.
                            Move pan off the heat and fluff rice with a fork, let sit with lid on unless ready to plate up.
